Cho Quan Chuck

Located in District 5, Ho Chi Minh City, Cho Quan Chuck is a vibrant and bustling area known for its rich cultural heritage and lively markets. The ward is home to a diverse community that celebrates various traditions and festivals throughout the year.

Residents of Cho Quan Chuck enjoy a variety of amenities, including parks, schools, and local businesses that contribute to the ward's unique charm. The area is also famous for its delicious street food, attracting both locals and tourists alike.

Community events and activities are regularly organized to foster a strong sense of belonging among residents. From traditional celebrations to modern gatherings, Cho Quan Chuck offers something for everyone.

Bau Sen Market

Bau Sen Market is a bustling marketplace located in District 5, Ho Chi Minh City. Known for its wide variety of goods, from fresh produce to clothing and household items, the market is a favorite destination for both locals and tourists. The vibrant atmosphere, combined with the friendly vendors, makes shopping at Bau Sen Market a unique and enjoyable experience.

The market is also famous for its delicious street food, offering a range of traditional Vietnamese dishes that attract food enthusiasts from all over the city. Visitors can explore the numerous stalls, sample local delicacies, and immerse themselves in the lively culture of the area.

Community events and activities are often held at Bau Sen Market, fostering a strong sense of community among residents and visitors alike.

Elementary School Bau Sen
