Activities of our ward

Community Clean-Up Day

Our ward recently organized a Community Clean-Up Day, where residents came together to beautify our neighborhood. Volunteers of all ages participated in picking up litter, planting flowers, and painting public spaces. The event fostered a strong sense of community pride and environmental responsibility among participants.

The success of the Clean-Up Day has inspired plans for future environmental initiatives, including recycling programs and green space enhancements. We are grateful to all who contributed their time and effort to make our ward a cleaner and more welcoming place.

Cultural Festival

Our ward recently hosted a vibrant Cultural Festival celebrating the diverse traditions and heritage of our community. The event featured traditional music and dance performances, art exhibitions, and a variety of food stalls offering local delicacies. Residents and visitors alike enjoyed the festive atmosphere and the opportunity to learn about different cultures.

The Cultural Festival not only provided entertainment but also promoted cultural understanding and unity among our residents. We look forward to making this festival an annual event that continues to enrich our community's cultural fabric.

Local Business of the residents

Our ward is proud to support a variety of local businesses owned and operated by our residents. From charming cafes and restaurants to unique retail shops and service providers, these businesses contribute to the economic vitality and character of our community. Many of these establishments prioritize quality, customer service, and community engagement.

We encourage residents to shop locally and support these businesses, as they play a crucial role in sustaining our ward's economy and fostering a sense of community. By choosing to patronize local businesses, we help create jobs and promote entrepreneurship within our neighborhood.
