About us

Welcome to the Dragon Fruit Family Ward website! We are a vibrant and diverse community located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City. Our ward is known for its rich cultural heritage, friendly residents, and a strong sense of community spirit.

At Dragon Fruit Family Ward, we take pride in our commitment to fostering a safe and inclusive environment for all our residents. We offer a variety of community programs and activities designed to bring people together and enhance the quality of life in our neighborhood.

Our mission is to create a welcoming space where everyone feels valued and supported. Whether you are a long-time resident or new to the area, we invite you to explore our website and learn more about what makes our ward special.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to connecting with you!
